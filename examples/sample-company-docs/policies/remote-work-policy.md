# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely when their manager approves the arrangement and the work can be performed securely.

## Requirements

- Use company-approved devices for company work.
- Store work documents only in approved company storage.
- Do not copy confidential data into personal cloud accounts.
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately.
- Use VPN or approved secure access tools when required.

## AI Usage

Employees may use approved AI tools only with data that is allowed for that tool. Confidential documents, credentials, customer data, private keys, and unreleased product plans must not be pasted into public AI systems.
